FAQ

Is there a student discount?

Yes! Student tickets provide all the benefits and access of a regular conference ticket, but are priced at $199 to be accessible for students. Learn more. A valid student ID is required at onsite check-in.

How do I register?

You can register for PR Advanced Breaking Barriers on our online registration page.

How much does it cost?
